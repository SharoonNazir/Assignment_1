Blog-1: Alto's Diet Plan

In simple words, the Alto will be 100kg lighter, currently clocking at 680kg. The move is to reduce the energy usage of the vehicles depending on their weight. Moreover, adding related technology gain is also part of the plans. Tatsuya Matsushita, an executive in charge of Suzuki’s automotive technology, informed, “If a vehicle is heavy, we also have to make the battery and motor larger to get a long range and good performance.” At an event on the automaker’s tech strategy, he added that small batteries and motor make it possible for the company to reduce the price of its cars.
